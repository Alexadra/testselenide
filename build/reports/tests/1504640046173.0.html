Additional Information

About

Made from lightweight (200g) advanced Polartec fabric, the Women's Glemsford Polartec Fleece Gilet is highly breathable, fast-drying and warm. The snug, high collar and drawcord hem reduce wind chill, while LYCRA binding on the armholes enhance comfort. Layer it over a shirt or under a weatherproof jacket for warmth on a shoot.

Composition

100% Polyester Double Velour Solid Polartec

Care instructions

Do not bleach
Do not iron
Tumble dry on low heat
Do not dry clean

Explore all features:

  • Polartec fleece keeps you warm and is highly breathable
  • Iconic Musto high collar to keep rain and wind off your neck
  • 2 side zipped pockets to keep items safe
  • Scooped back hem for added coverage and a hem adjuster to keep the weather out
  • Attractive brass trims and detailing
  • LYCRA bound armholes provide a snug, comfortable fit
